About us

The Best Hotel in West Africa

RayStones Exclusive Apartments is the most preferred hotel in West Africa, and it is all about the right mix! Located in the heart of Erunwon, we offer our clients a perfect blend of business & leisure amenities with dining and recreational options delicately infused in one amazing space. We crown all these with services that meet the highest international standards.

Our hotels are designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security is our primary concern and you will find our customer care second to none.
